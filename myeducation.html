Education

I went to Edwardsville High School, which is located in the small town of Edwardsville in Illinois. While I was there, I was involved in several extracurricular activities, including the school newspaper and the debate team. I also took a variety of classes, ranging from English literature to calculus.

After graduating from high school, I decided to pursue a degree in Information Technology with a minor in Business at the University of Missouri, which is located in Columbia, Missouri. The university is known for its excellent computer science program, and I've been fortunate enough to take courses in programming, algorithms, and data structures, among others. I've also been able to learn from some of the best professors in the field, who have helped me develop my skills and understanding of the subject.

During my time at the University of Missouri, I've worked on several projects, including beginning to developing a mobile app that helps students find free food on campus, creating a website for a local non-profit organization, and building a database management system for a small business.
